## Layout System
- **Full-screen pages** - Each section occupies entire viewport
- **Vertical page flow** with smooth transitions between sections
- **Mobile-first responsive** - Must work beautifully on phones and desktop

## Typography
- **Welcome screen**: Large, elegant serif or modern sans-serif for "Welcome Adil Mirza aka Mutoo ji"
- **Wishes page**: Readable, warm typography with comfortable line height for long-form blessings
- **Emphasis text**: Gold color for special words and names
- **Hierarchy**: Clear distinction between headings, body text, and signature

## Animations & Effects
- **Typing animation**: Letter-by-letter reveal for welcome
- **Fade/slide animations**: Staggered text entrance on wishes page
- **Bounce/glow**: Cake animation effect
- **Floating particles/sparkles**: Ambient animation throughout site
- **Smooth page transitions**: Fade or slide between sections
- **Audio fade-in**: Background birthday song with smooth volume curve

## Component Specifications

### Navigation
- **Scroll-triggered** or **click-triggered** page transitions
- Smooth scrolling or page-swap animation
- Optional: Subtle progress indicator

### Interactive Elements
- Clickable animated cake with hover glow
- Audio player with play/pause toggle
- Responsive touch interactions for mobile

## Spacing & Rhythm
- **Page padding**: Generous breathing room (p-8 to p-12)
- **Content max-width**: Centered containers for readability
- **Vertical spacing**: Large gaps between text blocks on wishes page

## Visual Effects
- **Gradient backgrounds**: Smooth black-to-purple transitions
- **Particle system**: Gentle floating sparkles (gold/purple/white)
- **Glow effects**: Subtle radiance on interactive elements and cake
- **Shadow depth**: Soft shadows for elevation and premium feel

## Tone & Personality
- **Premium & Elegant**: Sophisticated animations, refined color palette
- **Romantic & Heartfelt**: Warm messaging, personal touches
- **Playful**: Emoji usage, interactive cake, fun surprise reveal
- **Reverent**: Islamic blessings and prayers prominently featured
